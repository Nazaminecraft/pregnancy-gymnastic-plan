Gymnastic Plan

Pregnancy‑Safe Exercise Plan

Athlete: Thai woman, age 40 Gestational Age Anchor: 25 August 2025 (0 weeks 0 days) Equipment: treadmill • pool • bicycle • bands • dumbbells • mat
General guidance only — not medical advice. Keep intensity moderate (Rate of Perceived Exertion 4 to 6), use the talk test, stay hydrated, and avoid overheating.

How to Use This Plan

Structure

  • Three sessions per week. Spread them with at least one rest day between (for example, Monday, Wednesday, Saturday).
  • Do every item inside each block unless the header says “Pick one”.
  • Strength and core are presented as circuits. Complete the listed rounds and rest as noted.
  • Use the talk test and Rate of Perceived Exertion 4 to 6 for effort; slow down if you cannot speak in full sentences.

Execution Key and Defaults

  • Strength default: three rounds • eight to twelve repetitions • rest sixty to ninety seconds • tempo about two seconds down, no pause, two seconds up • exhale on effort.
  • Core default: two to three rounds • six to ten slow repetitions or fifteen to thirty second holds • rest thirty to forty‑five seconds.
  • Mobility default: two rounds • twenty to forty second holds or six to ten controlled repetitions • easy breathing.
  • Cardio default: twenty‑five to thirty‑five minutes at Rate of Perceived Exertion four to five; intervals when specified with work to rest.
  • Pelvic floor default: two by six repetitions of six to eight seconds gentle contractions; six to eight seconds full relaxation; breathe throughout.
  • Labels: Purpose (why) • Setup (start) • Movement (path and tempo) • Breathe • Avoid • Alternatives and Modifications.

Week 10 (Gestational Age 10 weeks 0 days to 10 weeks 6 days) — 2025‑11‑03 to 2025‑11‑09

Session 1 — Strength A (Lower emphasis) — 35 to 40 minutes — Rate of Perceived Exertion 4 to 6

Equipment: dumbbells, bands, mat • Alternative: full body‑weight

Warm‑up (Do all) — 6 to 8 minutes

  • Cat‑Cow × 6 to 8 cycles — video
    Purpose: gentle spinal mobility and back comfort.
    Setup: hands under shoulders; knees under hips.
    Movement: inhale to arch; exhale to round with slow control.
    Avoid: forcing end‑range or straining the neck.
  • 90/90 Breathing 2 × 5 breaths — video
    Purpose: diaphragmatic breathing and rib‑cage control; pelvic‑floor coordination.
    Setup: reclined or wall‑supported.
    Movement: inhale to expand ribs; long gentle exhale to draw ribs down.
    Avoid: shrugging shoulders or breath‑holding.

Main — Circuit A (Do all) — three rounds • rest 60 to 90 seconds

  • Goblet Squat to Box — 3 × 8–10 repetitions at Rate of Perceived Exertion 5; rest 75 seconds — video
    Purpose: lower‑body strength; sit‑to‑stand pattern; stable depth.
    Setup: feet hip‑width; dumbbell at chest; box at comfortable height.
    Movement: hinge and sit back to lightly touch box; drive through mid‑foot to stand tall.
    Breathe: inhale to lower; exhale to stand.
    Avoid: knees collapsing inward, bouncing on box, breath‑holding. Alternatives and Modifications: body‑weight box squat; raise box height as pregnancy progresses.
  • Dumbbell Hip Hinge (Romanian deadlift style hip hinge) — 3 × 8–10 repetitions at Rate of Perceived Exertion 5; rest 75 seconds — video
    Purpose: hamstrings and glutes strength for posture and back support.
    Setup: feet hip‑width; dumbbells by thighs; soft knees; shoulders down and back.
    Movement: push hips back keeping spine long; lower until hamstrings stretch; stand by driving hips forward.
    Breathe: inhale to lower; slow exhale to stand.
    Avoid: rounding back, locking knees, letting dumbbells drift away. Alternatives and Modifications: hip hinge with hands on hips; shorten range if back tightness.
  • Resistance Band Row — 3 × 10–12 repetitions at Rate of Perceived Exertion 5; rest 60 seconds — video
    Purpose: upper‑back strength and posture; counters rounded shoulders.
    Setup: anchor band at chest height; stand tall with ribs gently down; neutral neck.
    Movement: pull elbows back past ribs; pause one second; control the return.
    Breathe: exhale on pull; inhale on return.
    Avoid: shrugging, flaring ribs, leaning back excessively. Alternatives and Modifications: doorway towel row; seated row if balance needs support.

Finisher — Core and Anti‑rotation (Do all) — two rounds • rest 45 seconds

  • Pallof Press — 2 × 10 per side at Rate of Perceived Exertion 4 to 5 — video
    Purpose: anti‑rotation core stability with breath.
    Setup: stand side‑on to band anchor; slight knee bend; hands at sternum.
    Movement: exhale to press arms forward; hold one to two seconds; control the return.
    Breathe: long, easy exhale on press; soft inhale on return.
    Avoid: twisting torso, shrugging shoulders, breath‑holding. Alternatives and Modifications: half‑kneeling hold; lighter band; shorter lever.

Cool‑down (Do all) — 4 to 6 minutes

  • Thread the Needle 2 × 20–30 seconds per side — video
    Purpose: thoracic rotation mobility; ease upper‑back tension.
    Setup: all fours; neutral spine.
    Movement: slide arm under; rest shoulder and head lightly; breathe into upper back; switch sides.
    Avoid: forcing range or twisting low back.
  • Calf Stretch 2 × 20–30 seconds per side — video
    Purpose: ankle and calf mobility for walking comfort.
    Setup: step back with heel down; toes forward.
    Movement: lean forward until mild stretch; keep hips square.
    Avoid: bouncing or pain; do not collapse arches.

Pelvic Floor (Do all) — 3 to 4 minutes

  • Kegels: 2 × 6 repetitions — 6 to 8 seconds gentle contraction, full relaxation — video
    Purpose: pelvic‑floor strength and coordination.
    How: inhale to relax; exhale to gently lift/contract; hold 6 to 8 seconds; fully relax between repetitions.
    Avoid: squeezing glutes or abdominals; breath‑holding; excessive tension.

Session 2 — Cardio: Treadmill Incline — 30 minutes — Rate of Perceived Exertion 4 to 5

Equipment: treadmill • Alternative: outdoor shaded walk

Plan (Do all)

  • Incline walking 30 minutes at Rate of Perceived Exertion 4 to 5; 0–3% incline — video
    Purpose: low‑impact cardio; glute and leg endurance.
    Setup: lace shoes; set 0 to 3% incline; light rail touch only.
    Movement: short steps; upright posture; steady pace that passes the talk test.
    Breathe: easy nasal or nose‑mouth breathing; sip water as needed.
    Avoid: hard rail grip; over‑striding; overheating.
  • Optional cadence surges: four by 30 seconds a touch faster, 90 seconds easy between.

Mobility (Do all) — 6 to 8 minutes

  • Cat‑Cow × 6 to 8 cycles — video
    Purpose: mobilize spine post‑cardio.
    Setup: hands under shoulders; knees under hips.
    Movement: slow inhale and exhale with gentle arc and round.
    Avoid: forcing end‑ranges.
  • Half‑Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch 2 × 20–30 seconds per side — video
    Purpose: relieve hip‑front tightness.
    Setup: tall torso; tuck pelvis.
    Movement: shift forward slightly until mild stretch; breathe calmly.
    Avoid: arching low back or pushing into pain.

Pelvic Floor (Do all) — 3 to 4 minutes

  • Kegels: 2 × 6 repetitions — video
    Purpose: pelvic‑floor endurance and coordination.
    How: inhale to relax; exhale to gently lift; full release between repetitions.
    Avoid: glute or abdominal bracing; breath‑holding.

Session 3 — Strength B (Upper emphasis) — 35 to 40 minutes — Rate of Perceived Exertion 4 to 6

Equipment: dumbbells, bands, mat • Alternative: wall push‑ups, band rows, body‑weight

Warm‑up (Do all) — 6 to 8 minutes

  • Cat‑Cow × 6 to 8 cycles — video
  • 90/90 Breathing 2 × 5 breaths — video

Main — Circuit A (Do all) — three rounds • rest 60 to 90 seconds

  • Incline Dumbbell Press — 3 × 8–10 repetitions at Rate of Perceived Exertion 5; rest 75 seconds — video
    Purpose: pushing strength without prolonged flat supine.
    Setup: bench 30–45°; dumbbells at chest; feet flat.
    Movement: press up with slight arc; control down to touch.
    Breathe: exhale to press; inhale to lower.
    Avoid: over‑arching back; flared elbows; breath‑holding. Alternatives and Modifications: wall push‑up — video; increase incline as pregnancy advances.
  • Resistance Band Row — 3 × 10–12 repetitions at Rate of Perceived Exertion 5; rest 60 seconds — video
    Purpose: scapular control and posture.
    Setup: band anchored at chest height; tall spine; ribs down.
    Movement: pull to ribs; pause one second; slow return.
    Breathe: exhale on pull; inhale on return.
    Avoid: shrugging or craning neck. Alternatives and Modifications: seated row.
  • Goblet Squat to Box — 2 × 10 repetitions at Rate of Perceived Exertion 5; rest 60 seconds — video
    Purpose: reinforce lower‑body pattern.
    Setup: feet hip‑width; dumbbell at chest; box behind.
    Movement: control down to light box touch; stand tall.
    Breathe: inhale down; exhale to stand.
    Avoid: knees caving or rocking back. Alternatives and Modifications: body‑weight box squat; raise box height as pregnancy progresses.
  • Side Plank (knees) — 2 × 15–25 seconds per side; rest 30 seconds — video
    Purpose: lateral core and hip stability.
    Setup: elbow under shoulder; knees stacked; straight line from head to knees.
    Movement: lift hips and hold with steady breath; switch sides.
    Avoid: sinking shoulder or twisting. Alternatives and Modifications: elevated side plank; reduce hold time as needed.
  • Modified Dead Bug — 2 × 6–8 per side; slow; rest 30 seconds — video
    Purpose: core control with breathing; manage diastasis risk.
    Setup: on back with knees and hips at ninety degrees; ribs gently down; hands toward ceiling.
    Movement: exhale to extend opposite arm and leg; small controlled range; return on inhale.
    Avoid: doming or bulging, arching back, breath‑holding. Alternatives and Modifications: heel taps; shorten lever if doming.

Cool‑down (Do all) — 4 to 6 minutes

  • Thread the Needle 2 × 20–30 seconds per side — video

Pelvic Floor (Do all) — 3 to 4 minutes

  • Kegels: 2 × 6 repetitions — video
